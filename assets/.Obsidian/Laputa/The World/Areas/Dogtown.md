A town near the edge of [[Island of Laputa|Laputa]]

Namesake: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dogtown,_Massachusetts


Small community. people help eachother out but still feel the ennui of laputa. many people left during the last cycle and the community has shrunk as a result.

Dogtown was formed from a river which carved out a snaking canyon. The second era architects built a bridge based on the previous level of the river, but now the river has dried to a much lower level due to laputa's rotation in the winds.

Despite this, moderate wind still blows through the town, providing power to windmills which carry water out to the farm for storage in the water tower.
