
### Design Concept:
Revolver: The first basic gun, based on an air marshal's rifle, very shiny, very light, very cool

Glauncher: Grenade launcher similar to the TF2 one or what Dola uses in CitS, that one bloop tube

Mpistol: Skorpion for the style, one handed automatic fire, the basic automatic gun

Red7: Mauser, red9 resi reference, prototype gun that feels kinda broken, but gets more powerful as it levels up, spray and pray with infinite bullets, but only in bursts

Haymaker: experiment, makes enemies sleep instead of die? one shot one kill, but no reward. slow and methodical one shot at a time, stealth style

Blunderbuss: shoots physics birdshot in a spray, loud, inaccurate and powerful, good weapon to just run into enemies headfirst and blast em

Roberta: upgraded Mpistol, basically no downsides, late game automatic gun

Tsurugi: experiment, damages enemies in a short area in front of player. may be removed due to difficulty animating with other states. takes precise skill and timing to use properly

Speeder: like fireball in CS or jumping beans in Jazz jackrabbit, projectiles are stars that bounce around doing little damage but no need to be accurate. helps with pits of enemies

Turnstile Jumper: upgraded revolver doing slower shots but heavy damage, alternatively an experiment for a charging weapon. named after frums song/legendary cowboy
